FASHION PHOTOSHOOT BTS - 14TH JULY 2015


On Friday (23rd October 2014), it was our first day of photoshoot for our Major Studio Project and the session started from 9am until 6pm.
Our photoshoot locations were 2 classrooms combined; one for the photoshoot background and one for the make-up section.


As it took a couple of hours for the hairdressers and make-up artists to get the models ready, we proceed to take our group photos and self potraits.
For our photoshoot sessions we were divided into groups by our lecturer Joe so that we will know when would be our turn.


I wanted black smokey eye make-up and nude lips for my models whereas low sleek ponytail as I did not want the make-up to be overly exaggerated.
